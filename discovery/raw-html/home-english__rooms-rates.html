Overnight stay in Kiefersfelden - HOTEL ROOMS

Overnight stays with rich breakfast buffet

There are 80 beds in double or 3-bed rooms in the hotel. 4 single rooms. 25 rooms are easily accessible via the passenger lift. Rooms with connecting doors are also available for groups and families. Valuables can be deposited in the hotel safe free of charge. WLAN is free of charge.

All rooms with bath or shower/WC, single balcony, satellite TV, direct dial telephone, desk, wireless LAN.
