SHAPING THE SUMMER
Cycling through the Kaiser-Reich.
The Inn Cycle Path is very close to the hotel.
Experience the imperial era with a stop in Kieferfelden.
Perhaps you'll even stay a few days.
We'd
    be delighted if you'd stay with us.
Hiking and mountains in the Kaiserreich.
From the valley to the mountain to the summit: When hiking in Kiefersfelden, you get closer to the sky and enjoy fantastic views of
    the Inn Valley and the Kaisergebirge to Tyrol.
A wide range of tips where you can use your guest card free of charge, use offers and also help with the tour planning, the tourist office Kiefersfelden provides you.
Information on the mountain railways is also available on the websites of the Kaiser-Reich
www.kaiser-reich.com
Tour planning - Hocheck mountain railway in the neighboring village of Oberaudorf - Almen and huts in the Kaiserreich - Tour planning - Interactive leisure map - Local bus-hiking
    bus ....
More information about "MOUNTAIN EXPERIENCES":
www.kaiser-reich.com/alpin
