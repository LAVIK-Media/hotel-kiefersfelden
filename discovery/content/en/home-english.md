WARMLY WELCOME
Where Bavarian hospitality is at home.
A "Royal Bavarian Post Station" - that was in 1820. Today, the innkeeper harnesses his horses for the eventful carriage ride through the magnificent mountain landscape in Kiefersfelden only for
    his guests.
Hotel zur Post - that is Bavarian hospitality. Bavarian tradition is still at home here and is lived with all its warmth.
Whether in the hotel, in the guest rooms of the Gasthof zur Post or in the beer garden, there is always time for a personal conversation. The hosts Christine and Andi Pfeiffer are there for you,
    giving tips on events, holiday planning, excursions and places of interest.
It is not for nothing that the "white and blue chequered" banner of the Pfeiffer family hotel says: A "sound" experience in Bavarian cosiness - for every guest.
The Pfeiffer family looks forward to your visit
Overnight stays in Kiefersfelden
Holidays in Kiefersfelden in summer and and winter (all year
        round)
Buses and clubs - also with half board
...and everyone who loves good cuisine
Arrival is possible until 22:00 clock
The hotel is located directly at the train station Kiefersfelden.
By clicking on the DB symbol you are directly
    connected with
reiseauskunft.bahn.de
