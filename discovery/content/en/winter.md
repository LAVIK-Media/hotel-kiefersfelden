Winter Fun IN KIEFERSFELDEN
WINTER PLEASURES IN KAISERREICH - Around the Hotel zur Post there are plenty of different activities for your holiday.
A quick overview of some of the possibilities:
•    Leisure experience "INNSOLA" ~100m from the hotel
•    there are 2 bowling alleys (certified) in the hotel
•    horse-drawn carriage ride with the host (by arrangement)
•    Puppet show, Oktoberfest...
•    Wendelstein Rack Railway
•    Kufstein Fortress / Events
•    Beautiful winter hiking trails
•    Always freshly prepared cross-country ski trails
•    Winter toboggan run and much more
SKIING FUN IN KAISERREICH
HOCKECK: Ski slope with the longest floodlit area in Germany / NIGHT SKIING
SKI RESORTS: Sudelfeld, Oberaudorf, Wilder Kaiser Ski World
LIFT PASSES AND "INNSOLA" ENTRY TICKETS AT THE HOTEL/ask at reception.
BOOK ROOM
